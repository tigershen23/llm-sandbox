Integrations - Splash - Testing Event Emails

Below are steps to Test event Emails in Splash after the integration has been implemented

 

  1. Configure the event reminder email to include the unique link for the Welcome Event.
  2. Fill out the Event’s registration form with a test attendee. We recommend using an email account not associated with the Zendesk domain. (In the example below, we’re using a personal gmail address)
  3. Wait 5 minutes.
  4. (Optional) Export the Splash Guests to CSV to ensure that all attendees have unique links in the “Custom Field: Unique Link for Welcome Event” column. If some attendees do not have Unique Links, contact your Welcome Customer Success Manager. Note: Producers In Welcome do not get magic links for security reasons.
  5. Send a Reminder Email to the test attendee. Its unique link should be populated in the email. Please keep in mind that this is a test email, so please do not click “Send Test”, but follow the steps below
      • Click Email in the left-hand menu bar
      • Either click “new email” in the “Emails” tab, or choose a template in “My Templates” (For our example below, we chose “New Email”)

    • Design an email template that includes the unique link and the default registration link, example screenshot below. Once satisfied, click save and move onto Recipients.

 

    • Add the test attendee as a recipient, either manually or by clicking “Add Quick List
    • In the Review section, take one more look at the information and confirm everything looks good for the test. If it does, click “Send” manually at the top right of the screen. Again, it is important to follow these steps as opposed to “Send Test”, which can cause accidental emails to actual invitees.

 

6. If the emails do not come through as expected, contact your Welcome CSM. Below is a screenshot of a successful email reminder

 

 

7. When the time comes to send the 24hr and Join Now Reminder Emails. Follow the steps in this document to resend the emails you tested. https://support.splashthat.com/hc/en-us/articles/205934116-How-do-I-resend-an-email-
