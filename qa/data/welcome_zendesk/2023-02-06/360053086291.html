Help, my Attendees/Speakers are saying they can't get into the event!

If your attendees are having trouble entering an event you can utilize Welcome's One-Click Link to get them into the event easily. We have a full walkthrough on One-Click Links here.

Video tutorial:

  • Go to the Event Dashboard.
  • Go to the attendee section or speaker section.
  • Find the user:
    • If they aren't in there you can create an account for them. Just make sure to double-check if the person is who they say they are. You wouldn't want to accidentally allow someone into the event who shouldn't be there.
  • Go to the right-hand side where it says "Actions" and click on the three dots.

  • Click "Send One-Click Registration".
  • Tell the user to check spam/junk folders just in case.

Still need help?

If none of the solutions work above and you're still experiencing issues, feel free to contact support@experiencewelcome.com or click Help at the bottom right corner of the screen.
