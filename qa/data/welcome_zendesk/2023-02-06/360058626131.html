How to Export From Figma

After you click on one of our Figma links it will ask you to create an account, please do so.

Export in Bulk

  1. On the right sidebar, you will see an Export button
  2. It will ask you to confirm your selection with a pop-up
  3. Confirm your selection and click the "Export" button at the top right

Export Individual

  1. Select the asset you want to export (make sure to click the asset title to select the full asset)
  2. On the right sidebar, scroll to the bottom until you find Export
  3. Click Export, it will download the asset right away
