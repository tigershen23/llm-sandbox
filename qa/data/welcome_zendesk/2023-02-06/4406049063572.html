Custom Registration Guide

With custom registration, you are now able to set up custom fields to gather more information about your registrants. Custom registration will allow you to collect specific data based on the required fields you choose to implement. All this data will be available in the attendee tab either before or after the event (in other words, this data does not live in the post-event analytics).

Where to Customize your Registration Fields

You can access the Registration Fields by going to your Event Dashboard and clicking Registration in the navigation panel on the left.

Creating your Custom Fields

On the left, you will see where you create and modify fields and on the right, you will see an active preview of the registration fields. By default, you will see the first name, last name, email, company, and role (this is not changeable). First name, last name, and email are the minimum required fields to create a Welcome registration page and the latter can be enabled/disabled. 

Adding a Custom Field

Start by clicking on "Add a field" below "Custom fields" and you will be offered 3 field types.

Text Field

  1. Field label - Name of the Field.
  2. Placeholder (optional) - A viewable example for users.
  3. Data Field Name - Name displayed in the attendee CSV download data. This auto-populates with the Field label.
  4. Toggle for required field.

Dropdown

  1. Field label - Name of the Field.
  2. Placeholder (optional) - A viewable example for users.
  3. Data Field Name - Name displayed in the attendee CSV download data. This auto-populates with the Field label.
  4. Toggle for required field.
  5. Options - Fill out the different drop-down options for your users to select.

Checkbox

  1. Field label - Name of the Field
    1. You can add hyperlinks for users to view such as terms of service acknowledgments. 
    2. To add hyperlinks:
      1. Click + Add a hyperlink (link:`LABEL`[https://]).
      2. Replace "LABEL" with what you want to be displayed.
      3. Replace "https://" with your URL.
  2. Data Field Name - Name displayed in the attendee CSV download data. This auto-populates with the Field label.
  3. Toggle for required to check.

Once you have filled out the fields that you want and need. You can use the Preview section on the right to see your changes in real-time. When you have finished click "Save and Publish". Click "Registration Preview" at the bottom left to review the published changes.
