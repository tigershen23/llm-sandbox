Welcome back for another round of updates, fixes, and features!

Features

  • BETA: Skip Welcome Registration Form
    • We are making a setting for previously registered attendees to jump right into events, without needing to confirm their information. When we know an attendee's first name, last name, email address, and they don't need to put in any required custom fields, they should be able to get into the event without seeing Welcome's registration form or entering a password.
    • When a new attendee, who doesn't have a password, chooses "Register with Email" and "Login with Password Instead" they will still hit the Welcome Registration Form, even if Skip Welcome Registration Form is set.
