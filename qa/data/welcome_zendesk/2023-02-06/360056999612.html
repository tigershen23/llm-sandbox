One-Click Registration Link Guide

Our One-Click Links are a great way to get users into the event with the least amount of friction. Welcome makes it easy for you, the producer, to send them as a one-off to one person, to a group of people, and even be able to get the links and send them to your users in whatever personalized fashion you would like. 

One thing to note about One-Click Links is if it is generated before their associated event ends, the link will expire two weeks after the event ends.

Sending an Individual One-Click Registration Link

  1. In your event dashboard, go to either Attendee, Speakers, or Staff depending on the title of the individual you are looking to send the link to.
  2. Search for the individual by either using the search field at the top or scrolling through the list.
  3. Once you have found the individual, click on the stacked three dots on the right in the Actions Column.
  4. Click "Send One-Click Registration" and a pop-up will appear in green at the top right stating it has been sent.

Sending a One-Click Registration Link to a Group

Send One-Click Link to All

  1. In your event dashboard, go to either Attendee, Speakers, or Staff depending on the title of the individuals you are looking to send the link to.
  2. At the top right, you will see a button "Send Invites".
  3. Click on the button and then click "Send Invites" in the pop-up to confirm.
  4. This will send emails to all Unconfirmed user

Send One-Click Link to a Specific Group

  1. In your event dashboard, go to either Attendee, Speakers, or Staff depending on the title of the individuals you are looking to send the link to.
  2. Check the boxes to the left of the user accounts you would like to send One-Click Links to
    • You will not be able to the search bar; you will need to scroll through the list of people. 
    • At the bottom, you can expand the list view to up to 100 people.
  3. Once you have selected the individuals, scroll towards the top, and you should see a button "Send Invite(s)" to the left of a trash can.
  4. Click on the button and then click "Send Invites" in the pop-up to confirm.

How to Get a Copy of One-Click Links

Individual Link

  1. In your event dashboard, go to either Attendee, Speakers, or Staff depending on the title of the individual you are looking to send the link to.
  2. Search for the individual by either using the search field at the top or scrolling through the list.
  3. Once you have found the individual, click on the stacked three dots on the right in the Actions Column.
  4. Click "Copy One-Click Registration Link" and a pop-up will appear in green at the top right stating it has been copied.

Group of Links

  1. In your event dashboard, go to either Attendee, Speakers, or Staff depending on the title of the individual you are looking to send the link to.
  2. On the right above the list of individuals, you will see a cloud with a down arrow.
  3. Click it, and you will download a CSV file of all your users, and inside the CSV will have a column for each user, One-Click Link.
