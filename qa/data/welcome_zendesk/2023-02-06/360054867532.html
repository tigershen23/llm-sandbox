Creating Burner Accounts

In certain use cases, you may need to use burner accounts for users to test the platform or perform a "mock run of the show." Whatever it maybe we have a guide that will help you create burner accounts to do what you need.

Instead of going to Gmail and creating multiple accounts, you can use an email generating website to create email burner accounts that you can use to register for the Welcome event.

Step 1: Create a Burner Account

  1. Go to generator.email website
  2. Click "Generate New E-mail"
  3. Copy the email address and do not close tab/window

Step 2: Register Burner Account with Welcome Event

  1. In the Event Dashboard, add a burner email account as an attendee, speaker, or producer based on your needs

    1. You may need to send a "One Click Registration" after you add the user

  2. Go back to generator.email and scroll down, you will see the Welcome email to confirm registration

  3. Click the email and click on the "Register" link

  4. By clicking the link, it will take you to a new tab/window to finish the registration.

  5. Account has been created, and now you should be able to login with the burner email address.
