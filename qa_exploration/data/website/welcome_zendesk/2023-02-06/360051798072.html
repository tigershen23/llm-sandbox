10/19/20

Lounge & Breakout Rooms

These rooms have been getting some fresh paint! Here are a few upgrades to keep in mind when planning your next event:

  • Room limit increased. Lounge and breakout rooms now support up to SIXTEEN people. Woot!
  • Screenshare. Producers & Speakers can now share their screens in these rooms. So if you need to facilitate a discussion, go ahead and present those fancy slides!
  • Lounge room customization. Whether it's a sponsored room or a themed happy hour you can now add a logo, a brand color, a call-to-action button, and custom photobooth templates to lounge rooms.

Domain Switch

As part of some housecleaning we're switching our app domain name from app.welcomeonline.co to app.experiencewelcome.com, this will take effect on October 29th. Any previous links will still work for the next 6 months. For any future invitations, just use the new link. Also please make sure to whitelist emails coming from the experiencewelcome.com domain so you and your attendees never miss an email.

Compatibility Checker

Running kick-ass events from a web browser is no easy feat! VPNs, browsers, and connection speeds all play a role. So to make sure your speakers, attendees, and Oprah can shine on Welcome we've built a quick compatibility checker. Please give it a try and include it in your event communications!

Recording

We've increased performance to make recordings a bit more reliable! Just remember to click Start Recording at the start time of your event. ;)

Disable & Enable Chat

You can now toggle on or off any part of chat (i.e. Q&A, Help, etc) at any moment within you event.
