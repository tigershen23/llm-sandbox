How do I add more attendees, speakers or producers/staff?

Full video tutorial here:

From the event dashboard go to the section where you want to add people (attendees, speakers, staff)

 

Adding attendees and speakers

  • For attendees and speakers you can simply press the blue "add" button on the top right hand area

  • Note that first name, last name and email are required

  • You can also "convert" attendees (if they already pre registered) to speakers
    • Please note that there is a 100 person limit on Speakers. 

 

Adding producers/staff members

  • For staff you will need to contact us. Please fill out this form and we'll get back to you within 24 hours. If it's a very urgent need please email us at support@experiencewelcome.com and we'll get to it ASAP.
