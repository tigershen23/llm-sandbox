2/2/21

Mobile-viewing is now here!

Your attendees can join from anywhere using their mobile devices. They will simply need to register for your event and jump in using their smartphone or tablet to watch the mainstage live.

 

Viewing capabilities in Safari

Your attendees can also view your event using the Safari browser. Similar to the mobile-viewing experience, once registered, your attendees can open Safari and watch the mainstage live. 

 

Download assets from your event dashboard

You can download your event assets by navigating to your event dashboard, clicking stage media and locating the download button beside the desired asset.

 

We've gone worldwide with multi-language support

We now offer the ability for attendees to customize language. From registration to the live event, we support various languages for the full attendee experience. Interested in this feature? Contact your Customer Success Manager for more details. 

 

Set the mood of your event with music

Upload mp3's into your event and hit play as a speaker is getting introduced or just have it playing in the background, adjusting the volume levels as needed. 

 

We've made breakout rooms even better!

Pre-load all attendees to specific breakout rooms and add additional time to an open breakout session. Check out this video to see all of the latest capabilities.
