Lounges - A casual place for attendees to hang out with each other.

Breakout Rooms - Think of these as a more structured way to breakup attendees into smaller groups for live on-camera discussions. Breakouts can be automatically sorted or manually put together; plus producers can move people to different breakout rooms if needed. For a more detailed article please check out our Breakout Room Guide.
