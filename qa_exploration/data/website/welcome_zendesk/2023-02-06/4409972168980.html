Producer Quick Start Guide

Welcome to our Producer video series that's intended to quickly orient you with our different systems across the three user roles and the interfaces you will be using. Each section will have links to specific topic articles for you to deep dive into all the details. 

Before we dive into it let's ensure you have checked out our technical checks and recommendations. 

Key takeaways:

  • Setting yourself up for an awesome Welcome experience
  • Use Chrome and limit the number of open tabs and windows
  • Tablet/Mobile work, but we still recommend laptops/desktops for the full experience
  • Confirm your Browser and System permissions are set correctly
  • If you are unable to see video/audio on the stage check if you are on a VPN/firewall/security software that is blocking it
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
