Screen Sharing Presentations (1 screen)

While it is possible to screen share on one screen, we highly recommended that you use a second screen, one for the Welcome Green Room and the other for your presentation. If you are going to be doing that please go to our Screen Sharing Presentations (2 screens). We also have dedicated guides for each browser and device type combination:

  • Screen Sharing on Chrome Windows PC
  • Screen Sharing on Microsoft Edge Windows PC
  • Screen Sharing on Chrome Mac OSX

For Powerpoint

Video walkthrough here:

  • Go to "Slide Show", then "Set Up Slide Show"

  • Choose "Browsed by an individual (window)"

  • While still in "Slide Show", choose "Play from Start" on the left

  • Resize the PowerPoint to be about 2/3rds of your screen (might take some trial and error here depending on your screen size)

  • Click "Present" on Welcome (it will be towards the bottom left-hand corner)

  • Choose "Application Window" and then Powerpoint

  • From here, resize your two windows (PowerPoint and Welcome) into whatever is comfortable for you. Note that you'll still need to keep the PowerPoint window fairly big as if you try to make it smaller it will resize your presentation on Welcome.

For Keynote

Video walkthrough here:

  • First, please make sure you have the latest update. Otherwise, this might not work as intended.

  • Go to "Play"> Select "In Window" > "Play Slideshow" Please note that you cannot be in fullscreen in your browser for the Keynote window to show up in this section.

  • Resize the keynote to be about 2/3rds of your screen (might take some trial and error here depending on your screen size).

  • Click "Present" on Welcome (it will be towards the bottom left-hand corner).

  • Choose "Application Window" and then Keynote

  • From here, resize your two windows (Keynote and Welcome) into whatever is comfortable for you. Note that you'll still need to keep the Keynote window fairly big as if you try to make it smaller it will resize your presentation on Welcome. 

 

For Google Slides

Video walkthrough here:

  • Have your Google Slides presentation loaded up in a separate window from Welcome. Get invited and join the green room, then hit the "present" button on the lower left-hand corner.

  • Choose "Chrome Tab", then your Google Slides presentation. Hit Share. Please note that you can check the "Share audio" check box in the bottom left if you intend to include audio in your presentation.

  • Now go to your google slides presentation. You can either go to "view", then "present". Or you can simply press "present" on the upper right-hand corner. You can then resize/move the application window to your liking while also being able to see the Welcome platform. You can use arrow keys to change slides.

Screen Share Permissions

If you are screen sharing on a Mac for the first time please confirm that your system preferences are set correctly. If they aren't, you will receive a permission error preventing you from screen sharing.

  1. Go to Security and Privacy settings (Press CMD+ Space bar and type Security).
  2. Click the Privacy tab on the right.
  3. In the left sidebar scroll to the bottom until you find Screen Recording.
  4. Check the box for Google Chrome on the right.
  5. You will be prompted to restart Chrome, please do this.
  6. Go back to the event and jump in.
