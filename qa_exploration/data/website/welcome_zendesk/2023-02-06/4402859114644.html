Integrations Overview

What exactly is an integration?

Welcome can integrate with some of your favorite CRM/marketing tools (see below) to sync registrant data between the platforms. This makes it easier for registrants who sign up on the integration registration page to navigate and check into Welcome, with the intent to maximize attendance at your event.

  • If a guest registers in the integration, the guest's information will automatically be updated to include a unique link for seamless event access. This unique link can also be used as a personalized token in event reminders. 
  • If a guest registers on Welcome, the integration will catch that information and automatically transfer it over. 
  • Lastly, the integration will check for updated attendee information on a daily basis. It will update all guests with their most up to date registration status at that time.
