Where can I get data for my event?

Please navigate to the analytics section of your event dashboard. 

The analytics will update around 90 minutes after your event.

The following data is currently displayed

  • Attendance
  • A complete chat transcript
  • The Q&A
  • Polling 
  • Breakouts
  • Lounges

All of the data can be downloaded in individual CSV files. A link is provided on the top right hand corner.

 

Please note that multi track event data is NOT yet supported. Please email us at support@experiencewelcome.com for a complete report.
