1:1 Call Guide

1:1 Calls are supported in Welcome; you can attend jaw-dropping events and still chat with a new friend on the same platform. We want to make sure you are still connected to the event so you will have a picture in picture of the stage. This way, you can still have a close personal interaction with your new friend and commentate over what is being said on stage.

How to Jump into a 1:1 Call

1:1 calls can start in 1 of 2 ways, you either start the call or answer a call. Let's dive into both ways.

Starting a 1:1 Call

  1. Go to People tab and search for whom you would like to start a call with.
  2. Hover your mouse over the person, and you will see a button with a camera in it.
  3. Click the button, and you will be prompted with a Camera and Mic check to complete.
    1. If you are having permission issues, please refer to our troubleshooting article. 
  4. Once you click Join Video Call, it will take you into the room.

Answering a 1:1 Call

Answering through the notification pop up

  1. When someone starts a call with you. You will receive a pop-up on the top right corner of the screen stating, "(Person Name) is calling you"
  2. If you hover over the pop-up, you will see an option to "Join call" that you can click.
  3. Once you click Join Call, you will be prompted with a camera/mic check. 
    1. If you are having permission issues, please refer to our troubleshooting article.
  4. Once you click Join Video Call, it will take you into the room.

Answering through the DM tab

  1. Click on the DMs tab; you will see that a red dot will have appeared next to it.
  2. You should see the person listed who is trying to call you, click on the person and you'll see a Join call button.
  3. Click the button, and you will be prompted with a Camera and Mic check to complete.
    1. If you are having permission issues, please refer to our troubleshooting article. 
  4. Once you click Join Video Call, it will take you into the room.

What Does it Look Like in a 1:1 Call

1:1 calls look a lot like our lounges and breakout rooms with one special feature; the stage is still visible! This design creates a unique dynamic where you two are not only just speaking to each other but can also still participate in what's going on on the stage and commentate on it. You will still be able to engage in public chat, and you will have access to the Photo Booth feature where you and your friend can take a picture together.

 

As a Producer, How to Enable this Feature

Enabling this feature is pretty straightforward; you need to go to the Cogwheel at the top right corner in the Event Control Room and click the toggle for 1:1 Video calling.

Still need help?

If none of the solutions work above and you're still experiencing issues, feel free to contact support@experiencewelcome.com or click Help at the bottom right corner of the screen.
