Referral Program FAQ

Who is eligible for the referral program?

Anyone except Welcome Employees are eligible to submit a referral.

What Qualifies as a true referral?

The person you refer should have the ability and authority to make a decision about implementing Welcome at their organization.

Can I refer more than one person at the same company?

Yes, but it's best to refer the individual who would be the ultimate decision maker for implementing a virtual event software solution at their organization.

What if two people from my company refer the same person?

Yes, but it's best to refer the individual who would be the ultimate decision maker for implementing a virtual event software solution at their organization for either external or internal-facing events. This could include decision-makers in Marketing, Events Management, Internal Communications, HR or People Ops.

What does the person I refer need to do after I refer them?

Once we reach out by email and kick off the conversation, the person/company you refer must commit to an annual contract greater than $25,000 annually.

If my referral is accepted, how will I receive payment?

If your referral is accepted, we'll send you an electronic VISA card in the amount of $2,000 USD.
